About Pollen

Founded in 2010, Pollen has been Bangalore's premier destination for luxury floral arrangements and event décor. Located in the heart of JP Nagar and Jayanagar, we've been creating unforgettable moments for over 2500 satisfied customers.

Our team of expert florists combines traditional techniques with contemporary design to create stunning arrangements that speak to your heart. From intimate bouquets to grand wedding décor, we bring your floral dreams to life.
